Operation Hope is a non-profit with the goal of increasing financial literacy in the poverty-stricken communities in America. With over 110 locations across the country, they chose me to be one of the main developers of the website! I was excited upon hearing the good news but had no time to celebrate; time was against us.

I was working with two other colleagues of mine, Haroon Qahtan and Ammanuel Getenet. We assigned portions of the website accordingly and our deadline was the 2018 press release; that's when major funders(JP Morgan, Suntrust etc.) come and see what Operation Hope has done over the year and if they want to increase donations.
